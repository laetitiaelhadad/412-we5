About me

I'm Laetitia Elhadad, and I'm a student at Vanier College in Montreal, Canada, where I'm pursuing a degree in Micro-media and Hyper-publishing. Apart from my studies, I enjoy helping others. At Go Test Rapide, I assist clients with at-home Antigen tests and maintain client records. I'm passionate about making a positive impact in my community. I've worked with the Israel Cancer Research Fund to organize a dance show fundraiser, and I've choreographed routines for Herzliah High School's variety shows. In my free time, I volunteer at The Family Store and Mada, where I assist with providing support to families in need. I'm also a competitive hip-hop dancer, dedicating time to practices and competing in various dance competitions across Quebec. I'm bilingual in French and English, and I'm proficient in Microsoft and Adobe Suites. My interests include competitive dance, graphic design, social media content creation, and fitness. I'm committed to working hard, giving back to my community, and expressing my creativity in various ways.

This project involves a creative redesign of the label for the Alphaghetti product, focusing exclusively on typographic elements. This redesign aims to make Alphaghetti stand out on the shelves, amidst a sea of competitors.

La Semaine de la Francophonie Poster

The goal of this project is to create an eye-catching poster for La Semaine de la Francophonie at Vanier College. The key is to develop a visual metaphor that captures the spirit of Francophonie, combining creativity and understanding of Francophone culture. The design must effectively convey the event's theme and be adaptable for various formats, aiming to resonate with the college community.

Personal Monogram Logo Design

This project involves the creation of a unique monogram logo using your initials. The focus is on designing a distinctive and personal emblem that embodies your identity and can be used to brand your work. The challenge lies in intertwining the initials in an artistic and coherent manner, ensuring that the design is not only visually appealing but also represents your personal brand effectively. This monogram will serve as a signature stamp across your various works, providing a recognizable and professional touch to my work.
